CSE 180: Module 3

Ponder and Prove

Your Tasks

This week, we've learned about searching and are trying to solidify our understanding of Big O for an algorithm's efficiency (aka time-complexity or work per input size).

The task for this module is for you to provide 2 simplified algorithms for the class members to use for evaluating Big O efficiency. The 2 algorithms may be sorting, searching, or something from your friends' lives. These algorithms should be different than the ones you used for your Module 2 report. It would be helpful for you to keep in mind the inputs to the algorithm so that you can determine the Big O. You will provide 4 viable multiple choice answers with the correct one marked. Select the Big O answer that is appropriate.

Keep your algorithms to around 5 steps so that they can be displayed on one screen easily along with the possible answers.

You will need to have this submitted on Monday (or Tuesday for a Tu/Th class) so that we can use your questions on the next class period.
