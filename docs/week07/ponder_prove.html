CSE 180: Week 07

Ponder and Prove

Project Week!

As your project this week you will discover and appropriately describe algorithms others you know and love use in their lives. Select at least one interesting algorithm from each type of algorithm we have seen during the previous weeks (one per week). Describe each of these algorithms in deep detail as discrete steps of the overall algorithm. Be thorough in your investigations.

You are doing this in preparation for weeks 8 - 14 where you will attempt to measure the efficiency of these algorithms. Make sure your steps are actually describable and aren't composed of sub-steps and are not vague.

Write up each of the algorithms for the report to be shared with your group on Friday. Include the type of algorithm, how it is being used by the person you love, why they use it, and the discrete steps.

On Friday, come prepared to examine your group members' algorithm descriptions and help them see any hidden vagueness or missed sub-steps.
