CSE 180: Week 11

Ponder and Prove

Your Tasks

Goal this week: Explore how to use calculation to assess and classify greedy algorithms that somebody you know is using.

Remember young Gauss and how he cleverly added up the numbers 1 to 100? Your task this week is to teach someone you know and love how he did it, and in the process teach them the mathematical proof of the optimal efficiency of the greedy task-scheduling algorithm discussed in class.

How to Fulfill Return-and-Report Requirements

  • Make sure you take notes on the conversations you have outside of class.
  • Prepare to share with your classroom team your discussion notes and discoveries about the algorithms you investigated and assessed.
