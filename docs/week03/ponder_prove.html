CSE 180: Week 03

Ponder and Prove

Your Tasks

The life lesson this week is on the importance of and need for preparation, or what Dr. Stephen R. Covey (and others) have called saw-sharpening, which doing first makes sawing much more effective.

Ponder Doctrine and Covenants 88:119, and as commanded in the scriptures, liken it to yourself. One way of doing this is to substitute “life” for “house” since our lives need to be holy like the temple is holy.

Organize yourselves; prepare every needful thing; and establish a life, even a life of prayer, a life of fasting, a life of faith, a life of learning, a life of glory, a life of order, a life of God;

In class we are investigating the difference between disordered and ordered search. You will see how beneficial (even necessary) sorting beforehand is in order to take advantage of that difference. It reduces the time it takes to find something. Specifically, you are learning about how to use binary search to do this, as opposed to just looking at everything (linear search).

Your task this week is to figure out where you and your acquaintances are using “presorting” to improve the finding and deciding algorithms you use in your daily lives.

How to Fulfill Return-and-Report Requirements

  • Make sure you take notes on the conversations you have outside of class.
  • Prepare to share with your classroom team your discussion notes and discoveries about the algorithms you investigated.
