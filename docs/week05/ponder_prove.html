CSE 180: Week 05

Ponder and Prove

Your Tasks

Greedy algorithms, and especially greedy task scheduling, are the learning focus for this week.

The life lesson is to learn to better prioritize and execute tasks to minimize waste and staleness.

Your task is to figure out where you and your acquaintances have been using greedy algorithms to govern your lives.

How to Fulfill Return-and-Report Requirements

  • Make sure you take notes on the conversations you have outside of class.
  • Prepare to share with your classroom team your discussion notes and discoveries about the algorithms you investigated.
